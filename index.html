IMH Blow Technology

Leaders of the IMH™ Technologies

100% recyclable Integrally Moulded Handle (IMH™) PET Bottles and Manufacturing platforms.

IMH™ is a sustainable solution for bottlers and converters that allows for a simple transition to a single source PET or rPET handle bottle that is 100% recyclable and requires no separation in the recycling process. IMH™ bottles offer a commercially beneficial alternative to extrusion moulded, inserted handle and deep grip bottles in all applications suited to PET or rPET polymers. SDB and Integrated Plastics formed a development partnership in 2014 with the goal of improving IMH™ bottle and IMH™ preform quality, scale output capacity, reduce waste and lower overall manufacturing cost. This goal was realised with the commissioning of the world’s first IMH™ rotary blow moulder in Australia, supplying IMH™ bottles to all major grocery outlets nationally. Operated 24hrs 6 days a week since commissioning, the first IMH™ blow moulder is a testament to the quality and durability of SDB’s engineering and design works. SDB-IMH™ rotary blow moulders and injection moulders are technologically advanced, servo driven systems that ensure precise control and maximum levels of customization, in a reliable, efficient and easy to operate platform. As a leader in blow moulding and injection moulding technology in South Korea SDB understands the need for a strong after sales and technical support team. SDB carries the complete range of spare parts and components, ready for global dispatch at short notice and the English speaking team are always ready to assist customer enquiries both on-line and in person. SDB has an extensive portfolio of high-speed blow moulding and injection moulding equipment that goes beyond IMH™. Single cavity to 24 cavity rotary blow moulding machines and mid-sized, high speed injection moulders means SDB offers a solution for low, medium and high output requirements. Mould and product design specialist, Kisan Precision, works closely with SDB in the delivery of complete turn-key IMH™ and standard moulding systems that includes, design works, product development and optimisation, tooling, commissioning and mould servicing. Integrated Plastics, the owner and developer of IMH™ blow moulding technology, the world’s first 100% PET bottle with an integrally moulded handle.
